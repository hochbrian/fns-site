Friday Night Shindig

Hoch Haus

In a house on Spofford, there lived the Hochs. Not a nasty, dirty, wet house filled with the ends of worms and an oozy smell, nor yet a dry, bare, sandy house with nothing in it to sit down on or to eat: it was a Hoch Haus, and that means Friday Night Shindigs.

What is Friday Night Shindig?

The Shindig is about community. We want to bring people from all different circles together. It started as a way to offer a home cooked meal to airmen at the local Air Force base.
